help & introduction

introduction

BlockChalk is the voice of your neighborhood. Use it to talk to anyone, about anything.

BlockChalk is brand new and is in alpha. Go chalk up your block... then give us your [feedback]!

It's anonymous and there's no sign-up. Just stay calm and keep it legal.

getting started

Since this application uses your device's GPS system to determine your location, you may experience a short delay upon first starting up.

You'll see a loading spinner while the device gets a fix on where you are.

menu commands

Reset user ID
Request a new user ID from BlockChalk, in case you're seeing errors on startup or in case you'd just like to abandon your current random identifier. Note: After resetting your user ID, you'll no longer receive replies for your previous chalks.
Report GPS status
Mainly a debugging tool, this displays the current data reported to BlockChalk by your GPS unit.
