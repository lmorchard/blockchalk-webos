Instructions & About

BlockChalk is the voice of your neighborhood.

Use it to leave messages on your block, your street, at the coffee shop, or anywhere you happen to be. Respond privately or publicly to messages from people in your neighborhood.

People are already using BlockChalk to buy and sell stuff, chat with their neighbors, report crimes in the area and even find romancs. How will your neighborhood use BlockChalk?

Getting Started
